Tonight's Story
Adina and the Shy Cloud
Up in the sky, all the clouds were making shapes. One made a bunny. One made a boat. But there was one little cloud hiding behind the moon.
"Look," said Moonlight, pointing with her wing. "That cloud looks sad."
Adina gave the cloud a gentle pat. "That's okay. You don't have to be a shape. You can just be you." The cloud stopped shaking. Then — very slowly — it made a heart.
